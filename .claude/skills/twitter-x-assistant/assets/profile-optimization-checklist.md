## Profile Optimization Pro Tips

1. **First Impression Test**: Show your profile to someone unfamiliar. Can they explain what you do in 5 seconds?

2. **Mobile Optimization**: 80%+ of Twitter users are on mobile. Check how your profile looks on a phone.

3. **Social Proof**: Include numbers that matter (followers helped, revenue generated, years of experience)

4. **Evolution**: Your profile should evolve with your brand. Update it as you grow.

5. **A/B Testing**: Test different bio versions. Track profile visit ‚Üí follow conversion rate.

6. **Consistency**: Your profile should align with your content. Don't promise what you don't deliver.
